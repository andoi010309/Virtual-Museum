FAQs

What is the Admission Criteria of the school?

EVRSHS has stringent admission requirements aimed at selecting students:
* Required grades include:
a) Final grade of 85% in English, Science, and Mathematics.
b) Final grade of 83% in other subjects.
c) A weighted average of at least 85%.
d) Students must maintain a grade of 85% in major subjects and 83% in minor subjects to avoid probation or disqualification.

What curriculum does EVRSHS follow?

The school follows the Enhanced K to 12 Curriculum introduced by DepEd in the 2012-2013 academic year. The curriculum is enriched with additional subjects and electives prescribed by DepEd Order No. 49, s. 2003. EVRSHS offers Senior High School with a focus on the STEM academic track, emphasizing science and mathematics as core subjects.
